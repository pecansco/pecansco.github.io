Everything you need to know about our Company

Pecans’ IT solutions are tailored to power your business quickly, conveniently and dynamically for great customer experiences. Working as a one team, our collective brilliance is what gives us an edge. Our solutions are based on trust, and it’s essential for our success.

The values we live by

ONE TEAM

We work as one team. Taking great ideas and transforming them into reality is what we love to do as a one team. Different individuals working collectively for one mission is what makes us different.

CARE & RESPECT

Mutual care and respect in the workplace let all employees know that they are valued for their qualities, abilities and achievements. We treat our clients the same way we treat our team members - being treated respectfully helps to promote a positive work culture in which people are loyal, engaged, and motivated.

TRUST

We trust you, and we do our best to meet your requirements. Trust is the basis for everything we do and essential for our success as a team.

INNOVATION

We love to innovate - it's what allows us to learn, iterate, adapt and generate new ideas. Success is equally important. The will to innovate is what drives us forward, enabling us to deliver the energy of the future.

Our people are driven to be the best, to constantly challenge the norm to keep our team and Pecans at the forefront of software solutions.
